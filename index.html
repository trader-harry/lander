About Me

I’m a quant trader who also happens to love building stuff. I’ve spent a lot of time studying market behavior—how price moves, how OI and IV shift during the day, and how momentum shows up in the option chain. I’ve built systems that track real-time option data as soon as it refreshes on the NSE, and I use that to spot scalping and intraday setups. I’m also big on indicator-based strategies and do a lot of backtesting to make sure what I’m trading actually works in different conditions. For me, it's all about combining trading instincts with engineering logic to create smarter, faster, and more consistent systems.
